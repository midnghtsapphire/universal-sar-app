# MARKET_LABELS.md

## Purpose
Standard labels for shipping to market through repeatable issue/PR/release channels.

## Release Stage Labels
- `stage:discovery` — market/problem research
- `stage:pilot` — pilot implementations and validation
- `stage:ready-for-launch` — launch checklist complete
- `stage:ga` — generally available release

## Workstream Labels
- `stream:product` — product capability and UX
- `stream:ops` — deployment, reliability, on-call readiness
- `stream:security` — auth, vuln fixes, compliance
- `stream:docs` — collateral, runbooks, user enablement
- `stream:market` — positioning, campaigns, partnerships, SEO

## Buyer Segment Labels
- `segment:government`
- `segment:ngo`
- `segment:enterprise`
- `segment:volunteer-sar`

## Priority Labels
- `priority:p0-critical`
- `priority:p1-high`
- `priority:p2-normal`
- `priority:p3-low`

## SEO Execution Labels
- `seo:cluster-a-volunteer-rural`
- `seo:cluster-b-incident-command`
- `seo:cluster-c-terrain-missions`
- `seo:cluster-d-ai-drone-modern-stack`

## Definition of Done Label
- `market:ship-ready` — all deployment/runbook/checklist gates met and launch owner approved.
